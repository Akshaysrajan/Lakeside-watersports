    About Us

    Ours is a nationally and internationally recognized water sport organization which is having 30+ vessels including kayaks, powerboats, and sailboats and surfing boards.

    Having a handful of well experienced instructors who are certified from various water sports organizations, we offer you the safe and secure environment for your recreational and training activities.

    • RYA certified instructors.
    • RLSS and NIWS certified instructors.
    • YAI certified instructors.
